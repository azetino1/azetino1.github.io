1/2 Day Mountain Bike Tours

$140 per person
or
$120 per person with 2 or more people

Who: This ride is aimed at beginner to intermediate riders

Where: We will ride on gravel USFS roads and some double track with gradual inclines and descents.

How Long: Allow 4 hours for this adventure. Plan on biking for about 2 hours.

What is Provided: Full-suspension mountain bike, helmet, guide, transportation from Canyon Adventures

What You Need to Bring: Water, backpack (tell us ahead of time if you need to borrow one), energy bar, extra layer, rain jacket, sunglasses, sunscreen, sturdy shoes (no sandals)
